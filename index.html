Let's play ONIONHEAD!
  • Arrow keys to move (Left , Right and Up to Jump)
  • Collect all yellow stars to proceed to next level
  • Each level will have +1 more bombs
  • Press Any Key to Restart
  • (STAR) Gives You +10 points
  • (BOMB) You Die
  • (GOD MODE) You do not die for 10 seconds
  • (SLOW TIME) Time Slows Down for 5 seconds
  • (SUPER STAR) Gives You +30 points
  • All special stars vanish in 10 seconds
