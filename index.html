Let's play DONT KILL SANTA!
  • Arrow keys to move (Left , Right and Up to Jump)
  • Collect all white snowflakes to proceed to next level
  • Each level will have +1 more bombs
  • Press Any Key to Restart
  • (SNOWFLAKE) Gives You +10 points
  • (BOMB) You Die
  • (GOD MODE - RED SNOWFLAKE) You do not die for 10 seconds
  • (SLOW TIME - PURPLE SNOWFLAKE) Time Slows Down for 5 seconds
  • (SUPER STAR - BLACK SNOWFLAKE) Gives You +30 points
  • All special stars DO NOT GIVE POINTS and vanish in 10 seconds
